Mallory on Strike
by Ann M. Martin

Characters | Setting | Summary and Opinion

Characters

Mallory Pike
She is the eldest of eight children and the newest member of BSC (The baby-sitters club)
Kristy Thomas
She is the president of BSC. She has three brothers, one stepbrother, one stepsister and one stepfather.
Claudia Kishi
She is an artist that hates math. She is the vice officer of BSC.
Mary Anne Spier
She is very quiet, shy and very sensitive. She is the secretary of BSC.
Stacey McGill
Stacey is diabetic and very stylish. She moved to Stoneybrook, Connecticut from New York City because of her diabetes.
Dawn Schafer
She is the second newest member of BSC. Her mother, and Mary Anne’s father are dating.
Jessica Ramsey
She is Mallory’s best friend and the newest member of BSC (same as Mallory).
The Pike Kids
The Pike Family is really big. The members of Pike family are the girls Mallory(11) Vanessa(9), Margo(7) and Clarie(5) and the boys of Pike are the triplets(Jordan, Adam and Byron who are ten)and Nicky(8) (plus parents).
Pike parents
Their names are John and Dee Pike.
